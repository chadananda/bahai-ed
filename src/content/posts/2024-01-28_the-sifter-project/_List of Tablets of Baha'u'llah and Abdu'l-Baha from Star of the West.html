Tablets of Baha'u'llah and `Abdu'l-Baha in Book 1 of the 1978 reprint of Star of the West by George Ronald. Book 1 contains all of Vol.1 and Vol.2 to Number 11, i.e., March 1910 to September 1911

      This is a selection based on my own interests, not a comprehensive electronic version. It is intended primarily as part of a broader project of preparing searchable electronic versions of all of 'Abdu'l-Baha's works which have been translated in English. I have however included some pilgrims notes and reports relating to `Abdu'l-Baha. The spelling in the original is often idiosyncratic, but I have corrected only a few obvious typsetting errors. Corrections, and additional material which I have omitted, would be welcome.
                                    Sen McGlinn, 7 September 1998
                                    S.Mc_Glinn@Thuisnet.LeidenUniv.NL
Note from editor: I have added some basic formatting to this collection of Tablets to ease their online use. I have added a table of contents linked to the individual Tablets, and as well have attempted to isolate "titles" for each Tablet and place these "titles" in boldface and larger font. Neither of these formatting styles exist in the original documents, and the following Table of Contents headers are not native to the original text. -J.W
